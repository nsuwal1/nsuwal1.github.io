# Selected Projects

## Explainable Machine Learning for Norovirus Outbreak Risk

Development of an explainable machine-learning early-warning framework for oyster-associated norovirus outbreak risk using hydroclimatic, oceanographic, and geospatial data.

## Streamflow Prediction in Himalayan Watersheds

Application of artificial neural networks and other machine-learning methods for streamflow prediction in data-scarce river basins.

## Climate Change and Hydropower

Assessment of future streamflow and hydropower production under changing climatic conditions in Nepal.

## Flood and Hydraulic Modeling

Application of HEC-HMS, HEC-RAS, GIS, and field data for watershed modeling, river-hydraulic assessment, and flood-inundation mapping.

## Remote Sensing of Coastal and Inland Water Systems

Use of satellite data, GIS, and Google Earth Engine to evaluate hydroclimatic and water-quality processes.
